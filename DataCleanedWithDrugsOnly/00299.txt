Stardawg, also known as "Stardog," is a hybrid marijuana strain believed to be
named after the bright, sparkling crystal trichomes that blanket the strain
like stars. Made from a cross between Chemdog 4 and Tres Dawg, Stardawg is
beloved for its earthy pine flavors with sour undertones of diesel. The
effects of Stardawg are uplifting and may help medical marijuana patients
treating symptoms associated with stress, fatigue, and anxiety disorders.
Stardawg flowers in 67 days indoors and produces moderate yields.
