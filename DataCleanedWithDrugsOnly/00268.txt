Etizolam is a thienodiazepine derivative which is a benzodiazepine analog. The
etizolam molecule differs from a benzodiazepine in that the benzene ring has
been replaced by a thiophene ring and triazole ring has been fused, making the
drug a thienotriazolodiazepine.
